# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

### Phase 1: Immediate (0-3 months)
1. **GitHub**
   - **Rationale**: GitHub is the most widely used platform for software development, making it a natural fit for a quality management tool.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Unlimited public repositories, 500MB storage for private repositories.
   - **Integration Effort**: S
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of tracking code quality metrics and enforcing quality gates in the CI/CD pipeline.

2. **Jira**
   - **Rationale**: Jira is a popular project management tool used by development teams to track issues and manage projects.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Up to 10 users for free.
   - **Integration Effort**: M
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of linking quality issues to project tasks and tracking quality improvements over time.

### Phase 2: Short-Term (3-6 months)
3. **SonarQube**
   - **Rationale**: SonarQube is a widely used static code analysis tool that complements quality management by providing detailed code quality metrics.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Community edition is free with limited features.
   - **Integration Effort**: L
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of integrating static code analysis results into the quality management dashboard.

4. **Slack**
   - **Rationale**: Slack is a widely used communication platform that can be used to notify team members about quality issues and updates.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free for small teams with limited message history.
   - **Integration Effort**: S
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of real-time notifications and collaboration around quality issues.

### Phase 3: Medium-Term (6-12 months)
5. **Docker Hub**
   - **Rationale**: Docker Hub is a popular repository for container images, and integrating with it can help manage the quality of containerized applications.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Limited to 1 private repository with 200MB storage.
   - **Integration Effort**: M
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of ensuring the quality of container images used in the development process.

6. **AWS CodePipeline**
   - **Rationale**: AWS CodePipeline is a continuous delivery service that can benefit from quality management integration to enforce quality gates in the deployment process.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free for the first 12 months with limited usage.
   - **Integration Effort**: L
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of integrating quality checks into the CI/CD pipeline for AWS-based projects.

### Phase 4: Long-Term (12+ months)
7. **New Relic**
   - **Rationale**: New Relic is a performance monitoring tool that can provide valuable insights into the quality of applications in production.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier with limited features and data retention.
   - **Integration Effort**: L
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of monitoring application performance and linking it to quality management metrics.

8. **Datadog**
   - **Rationale**: Datadog is another popular monitoring tool that can help track the quality of applications in production.
   - **Free-Tier Limits**: Free tier with limited features and data retention.
   - **Integration Effort**: L
   - **Value-Add**: Solves the job of monitoring application performance and linking it to quality management metrics.
